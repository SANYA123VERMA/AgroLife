Horticulture

Scientific cultivation of fruits, trees, flowers, and ornamental plants.

What is Horticulture?

Horticulture is the science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, medicinal plants, and trees in a planned and scientific way.

It goes beyond traditional farming by using techniques like grafting, pruning, greenhouse farming, drip irrigation, and organic cultivation to improve plant quality and sustainability.

Pomology (Fruit Science)
What is Pomology?

Pomology is the study of fruit crops such as mango, banana, apple, citrus, and grapes. It focuses on improving fruit quality, yield, and disease resistance.

Arboriculture (Tree Science)
What is Arboriculture?

Arboriculture is the scientific study and management of trees, shrubs, and woody plants. It includes planting, pruning, protection, and sustainable maintenance of trees in farms, orchards, parks, roadsides, and urban landscapes.

What is Floriculture?

Floriculture is the cultivation of flowers and ornamental plants such as roses, lilies, orchids, and chrysanthemums.
