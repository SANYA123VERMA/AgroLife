Farm Machinery

Modern machines that make farming faster, efficient, and productive.

üöú What is Farm Machinery?

Farm machinery refers to mechanical equipment used in agriculture to perform farming operations efficiently. These machines help in land preparation, sowing, irrigation, harvesting, and post-harvest processing.

The use of modern farm machinery reduces manual labor, saves time, improves productivity, and helps farmers achieve better crop yields with less effort.

üå± Importance of Farm Machinery

Why modern machines are essential in agriculture.

Saves Time

Machines perform tasks much faster than manual labor.

Reduces Cost

Reduces dependency on human labor and long-term farming costs.

Higher Productivity

Helps in better crop management and increased yield.

üöú Types of Farm Machinery

Common machines used in agriculture.

Tractor

Used for plowing, tilling, hauling, and powering other farm implements.

Plough

Used for loosening soil and preparing land for cultivation.

Harvester

Used for cutting and collecting mature crops efficiently.

Seeder

Used for sowing seeds uniformly in the field.

Sprayer

Used for spraying fertilizers and pesticides on crops.

Thresher

Used for separating grains from harvested crops.
