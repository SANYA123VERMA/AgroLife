Sustainable Poultry Farming

Scientifically managed poultry farming that ensures healthy birds, high-quality eggs and meat, and profitable farming for rural entrepreneurs.

What is Poultry Farming?

Poultry farming is the practice of raising domesticated birds such as chickens, ducks, turkeys, and geese for the production of meat and eggs. It is one of the fastest-growing sectors in agriculture due to its short production cycle, high demand, and economic viability.

Modern poultry farming integrates traditional knowledge with scientific methods such as controlled housing, balanced nutrition, biosecurity measures, and veterinary care to improve productivity while ensuring animal welfare and food safety. It plays a vital role in meeting global protein demand and supporting rural livelihoods.

Key Components of Poultry Farming

Housing

Well-designed poultry houses with proper ventilation, temperature control, lighting, and space management help maintain bird health, reduce stress, and prevent disease outbreaks.

Nutrition

A scientifically formulated diet containing proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals is essential for rapid growth, strong immunity, and high egg production.

Health Care

Regular vaccination, cleanliness, disease monitoring, and timely veterinary intervention are crucial to prevent infections and maintain flock productivity.
