Livestock Management

Scientific care, breeding, and management of farm animals for sustainable farming.

üêÑ What is Livestock?

Livestock refers to domesticated animals raised in an agricultural setting for food, fiber, labor, and other products. Proper livestock management ensures animal health, productivity, and sustainable farming.

It includes activities like feeding, breeding, healthcare, shelter management, and disease control. Good livestock management helps farmers increase income and maintain environmental balance.

üîë Key Components of Livestock Management

Essential aspects of animal care and farming.

Nutrition

Providing balanced feed, clean water, and proper supplements to ensure healthy growth and productivity.

Healthcare

Regular vaccination, disease monitoring, and veterinary check-ups to prevent illnesses.

Shelter

Clean, ventilated, and safe housing to protect animals from extreme weather and predators.
